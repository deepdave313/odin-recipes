Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza is crispy on the outside, soft on the inside, and loaded with rich flavors. It's a simple and delicious way to enjoy a classic favorite right from your own kitchen.

Perfect for family dinners, gatherings, or a cozy night in, this recipe will give you a pizza with the perfect balance of sauce, cheese, and toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) instant yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and olive oil. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Add flour and salt to the mixture and knead into a smooth dough.
  4. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes until it doubles in size.
  5. Roll out the dough into a round shape and place it on a baking sheet.
  6. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  7. Sprinkle cheese and your desired toppings.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and enjoy!
